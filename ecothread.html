UX Case Study

EcoThreads

Sustainable Fashion Discovery Platform

Role

UX/UI Designer

Tools

Figma, Canva

Problem Statement

Many consumers want to make sustainable fashion choices but face significant barriers in their journey. The current market lacks a user-friendly platform that effectively connects eco-conscious consumers with verified sustainable fashion brands while providing transparent information and affordable options.

User Research

Research was conducted through extensive analysis of Reddit threads and online communities focused on sustainable fashion, revealing key user pain points and needs.

Key Findings

  • Most sustainable fashion brands offer unfashionable, boxy designs
  • High costs make sustainable fashion inaccessible
  • Limited accessibility to thrift stores and secondhand options
  • Difficulty finding right sizes in secondhand clothing
  • Challenging to verify genuine sustainability claims
  • Poor filtering and search capabilities on existing platforms
  • Difficulty maintaining personal style while being sustainable
